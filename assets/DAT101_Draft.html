Welcome to Spotify Unwrapped! Explore trends and popularity of your favorite songs and artists from January–March 2024. The dashboard helps you understand how audio features like danceability, energy, and tempo impact music popularity.

Use the controls to select a song/artist, pick a date range, and interact with visualizations. The choropleth map shows country-level popularity, while the line graph tracks trends over time.

Dive deeper into audio features with the bar chart, and uncover correlations between them using the scatter plot.

Choropleth Map (Popularity by Country)

The choropleth map visualizes the popularity of the selected song or artist across countries. Darker shades indicate higher popularity. This map dynamically updates based on your selection and is linked to the line graph for contextual trends.

Line Graph (Trend of Audio Features)

The line graph shows the trends of audio features like danceability, energy, and tempo, comparing week-to-week or month-to-month changes from January–March 2024. This feature helps to analyze the evolution of song or artist characteristics and their relationship with geographic popularity.

Bar Chart (Popularity by Audio Feature)

The bar chart visualizes the average popularity of the selected song or artist based on the chosen audio feature. This helps you understand which features contribute most to the popularity during early 2024.

Scatter Plot (Correlation Between Audio Features)

The scatter plot reveals the correlations between audio features (e.g., Energy vs. Danceability). It includes a color gradient to encode an additional variable, such as popularity or another audio feature.
